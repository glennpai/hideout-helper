Hideout Helper

What's this?

This is Hideout Helper, an app to help track "junk" items as you upgrade your hideout in Escape From Tarkov.

Cool, how do I use it?

Using Hideout Helper is like creating a grocery list.

You select the items you want to track by adding a Module to your cart.

Tracked Modules' cart icons turn green when they're being tracked.

Once a Module is being tracked, its requirements are shown in your Shopping List.

You can track as many or as few Modules as you want.

You can change the level of the Module you're tracking using the Up and Down arrows to the right of a Module's item list.

As you collect items on your Shopping List, you can click the item in the Module to mark it as "complete".

As you complete your items, your Shopping List will update indicating which items you do and don't need.

So... where do I start?

Start by setting each Module to the level appropriate for your Hideout and completing the appropriate items.

Once you're finished, Save your Hideout using the "Save" button in the nav bar.

Your changes are automatically saved when you make a change after not having saved during the last 5 minutes.

You can also import or export a Hideout code to save and share Hideout configurations with your friends.
